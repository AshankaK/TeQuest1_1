TeQuest

We all need some form of outside assistance in our daily lives.

It can be something as easy as changing a light bulb to something as changing the interior of your house or even as organizing a party or something more complicated as setting up a nuclear plant (joking). But the point is not everyone can do everything and not everything can be done by everyone.

We always need some help from someone else who can do the job, and do it better than us. TeQuest - Request a Technician is our way of assisting you in completing your tasks and making your life easier so you can spend your time on enjoying other things.

MarketPlace

We bring together businesses and people who need the service of these businesses and make your life easier.

Responsive/Interactive Design

The website is interactive, and easy to use. We plan to make it accessible if time permits

Web Security

Your information is safe with us. We don't collect any payment information so you can rest assured. You can verify the businesses registered with us before you hire them.

Project Specifications

  • Users can register as service providers or service consumers. Alternatively, we provide Guestuser access but with limitations (e.g No access details to discounts and other offers)
  • Businesses – big/small/freelancers any service sector who wishes to expand their reach to customers locally or globally can register with the website. They need to provide information on the business, their products and price for products and services and any discounts, freebies or any other offers they have.
  • Live Tracking – Progress Tracker – Urgent requests with local service providers are tracked and an estimated time of arrival is shown.
  • TeQuest – Registered Users have access to creating their own requests for their custom projects/tasks. They can set the price and time duration and also other details about their project. Businesses can check these TeQuests and respond if they are interested in taking up the project.

Functions

  • New user registration as a service provider or user
  • Separate logins for service providers & users
  • Local and Global options - users can request non local service providers if the request is not urgent and has a longer time frame for completion
  • Tracking function - Progress tracker - to track the progress of the technician if local and urgent (example food delivery or shipping)
  • TeQuest - Users can request a specific service provider or they can put up a service request as to what they need and service providers can respond
  • Feedback / Review - Registered Businesses and Users can submit their feedback on their experience using the site and the other party with which they worked/contracted.
